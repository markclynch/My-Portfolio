CannaClima

Full stack developer

CannaClima is a side project I'm working on to help a small business in Oregon control the business logic in their greenhouses to boost both production and productivity.

Using MongoDB, GraphQL, Express, Apollo, React and Node. Besides that I'm using some libraries to link the IoT devices to the cloud for ease of control and reporting. It's been an amazing experience I wouldn't trade for anything.

After finishing the front end developer path with Free Code Camp I moved on to Team Treehouse to get more experience in things like SASS, React, Express and Nodejs.
